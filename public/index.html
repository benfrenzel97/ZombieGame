⚙ HOW IT WORKS

1
SCOUT & EXTRACT
Each day a new looting zone opens — mall, hospital, military, or suburb. Read the scout report at the terminal, then enter the zone to scavenge weapons, ammo, scrap, and parts. The clock is ticking.
2
RUN BACK BEFORE DUSK
When extract begins you have 30 seconds to make it back through the corridor. Anyone caught outside when night falls is locked out for the entire horde.
3
DEFEND THE BASE
Build barricades, place turrets, and hold the exit corridor as the night horde swarms in. Survive 60 seconds and you can sleep through to dawn — or fight the full night for a jackpot weapon.
4
UPGRADE THE WORKSHOP
Spend parts at the workshop for permanent upgrades — bigger stash, reinforced walls, sentries, faster respawn, better starting guns. Plan for the long run.
5
DEATH IS REAL AT NIGHT
Day deaths respawn quickly. Night deaths drop you into spectator mode until dawn. If everyone dies during the horde, the run resets to Day 1.
